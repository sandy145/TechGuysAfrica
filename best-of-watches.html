Best of Digital Watches

Posted by Edmond sarpong on June 16, 2017

The advent of the smartphone has surprisingly not diminished the appeal of the watch. Watches continue to be one of the favorite choices for gift- seekers and desired component for the ‘fashionista’.

Today’s watch lover craves in a watch a device which does not only form part of a fashion statement but also a device which enhances the ability to interact with the world at the touch of a button.

The diversity of watches on display in present times is an attempt to satisfy the huge appetite of consumers for devices which meet their desires for sophistication and affordability.

Choosing a watch these days has become increasingly tough, due to plethora of reasons. Some of the reasons being the cost, the operating system (Android, IOS, Tizen OS etc.) and most importantly the design. However, TechGuys Africa, have compiled a list of the top 10 Smartwatches which seeks to meet the demands of the consumer and their associated prices.

Today’s watches gives you fresh tricks on your wrist enabling you to perform tasks such as, track your fitness, use GPS, browse your phone using your voice on the watch, read notifications and top breaking news headlines and more.

When choosing a smartwatch, the first thing you may want to consider is the functionality. How well does the watch sync with your phone as well as other phones? How good is the battery life? By and large, the decision comes down to how much you would be willing to spend, to have a watch, which meets your “smart” needs, on your wrist.
